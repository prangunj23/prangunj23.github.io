Coronavirus Screenings

If you have any of the following symptoms, please schedule a time to come to any of our drive-in screenings in order to get tested for the Coronavirus.

  • Fever or chills
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
  • Fatigue
  • Muscle or body aches
  • Headache
  • New loss of taste or smell
  • Sore throat
  • Congestion or runny nose
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Diarrhea
